Conclusion
The Sunshine/Moonlight architecture is operational end-to-end for a playable TXR session. It runs the complete Windows game and graphics stack on the Windows host and streams video/audio/input to the paired MacBook. This is separate from the unfinished research goal of running the game process on a CPU-only client while virtualizing D3D12 execution onto Google Colab.
